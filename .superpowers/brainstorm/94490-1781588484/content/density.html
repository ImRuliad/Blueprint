Compact — DataGrip Energy

~22px row height. Dense filter chips. Inline status bar. Every pixel shows data. Power users will love it — you see 30+ rows without scrolling. Trade-off: can feel overwhelming on first open.

Comfortable — Postico Energy

~36px row height. More breathing room. Rounded pill tabs. Fewer visible rows (~15) but each row is easier to scan. Friendlier on first open, still productive. Trade-off: power users may wish it was denser.

This primarily affects the data grid (where you spend 80% of your time), but the density choice cascades to the sidebar, tabs, and other panels too.
